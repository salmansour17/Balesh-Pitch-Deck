Chapter 02 / The Hard Truth 04 / 14

Then we ran our first real summer.

Operating live exposed two problems that would break the business at scale.

PROBLEM 01
Seasonality

Internship demand spiked in summer and collapsed the rest of the year. A purely student-internship model couldn't sustain steady revenue.

PROBLEM 02
Manual filtering

Screening interns for companies was painfully manual. Endless hours, effort, and resources per role. It didn't scale, and companies hated doing it themselves.

Balesh
